Our Story

Prestiage Catering and Events was started in 2019 by a passionate home cook who belived that great food should be availble at every kind of event, no matter the size or budget. What begun as a small home based catering opertion in Pretoria, quickly grew through word of mouth and a reputation for consistency and quality.

Today,we are a team of 8 dedicated proffessionals who regularly take on large scale events across Pretoria. From intimate Family gatherings to corporate conferences and wedding receptions, we bring the same level of care and attention to every single table we set.

Our strenght has always been the relationships we build with our clients. Many of our customers have been with us since the beginning and continue to trust us with their most important events.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, memorable catering experiences for every occasion, regardless of size or budget.

Our Vission

To become the most recognised cateringbrand in Pretoria by 2028, known for excellence, reliability and outstanding service.
